Pizza Napoletana

Description

A traditional Italian pizza with a thin, chewy crust, simple tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and basil, baked at high heat until lightly charred.

Ingredients

  • 500 g strong flour (tipo “00” if available)
  • 325 ml water
  • 10 g salt
  • 3 g fresh yeast (or 1 g dry yeast)
  • 300 g canned San Marzano tomatoes
  • 200 g fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour, yeast, water, and salt into a smooth dough. Knead 10 min. Rest, covered, 2 hours.
  2. Divide into 2–3 balls, rest again 6–8 hours (fridge overnight works).
  3. Blend tomatoes with salt for sauce.
  4. Preheat oven to highest setting (250°C / 480°F). If possible, use a pizza stone.
  5. Stretch dough by hand, top with tomato sauce, mozzarella, basil, and a drizzle of olive oil.
  6. Bake 7–10 min until crust is golden and slightly charred. Serve immediately.
