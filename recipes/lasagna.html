Lasagna

Description

A simple, comforting lasagna with layers of pasta, tomato-meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese, baked until golden and bubbling.

Ingredients

  • 250 g lasagna sheets
  • 400 g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (400 g) chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 40 g butter
  • 40 g flour
  • 500 ml milk
  • 100 g grated cheese
  • Salt, pepper, olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook onion and garlic in olive oil, add beef and brown.
    Stir in tomato paste and chopped tomatoes, season with salt and pepper.
    Simmer 15 min.
  2. Melt butter, whisk in flour, add milk gradually, cook until thick.
    Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grease a baking dish. Layer pasta, meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese.
    Repeat.
  4. Bake at 190°C (375°F) for 30–35 min until golden.
    Rest 10 min before serving.
