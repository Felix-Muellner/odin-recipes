Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A Roman pasta classic with spaghetti, crispy guanciale, egg yolks, pecorino cheese, and black pepper â€” creamy without cream.

Ingredients

  • 400 g spaghetti
  • 150 g guanciale (or pancetta if unavailable)
  • 3 egg yolks + 1 whole egg
  • 70 g grated Pecorino Romano (or Parmesan if needed)
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
  2. Dice guanciale, fry in a pan until crisp. Remove from heat.
  3. Whisk egg yolks, whole egg, cheese, and black pepper in a bowl.
  4. Drain pasta, add to pan with guanciale. Toss well.
  5. Off the heat, stir in egg-cheese mixture quickly, adding a little pasta water to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.
